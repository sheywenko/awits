Speakers

Jennifer J. Salau

Director of Partnerships & Tech Sales - Retention Concierge

Jennifer is passionate about African development through technology and collaboration. She is an international speaker featured in conferences like Digibbank, World Financial Innovation Series, Linkedin Local, and Customer Experience Africa. She currently serves as the Director of Partnerships and Technology Sales at the Retention Concierge an International award-winning boutique Technology Sales and Consulting company that is fast becoming Africa's number-one brand for attracting global technology partnerships that drive digital transformation efforts on the continent.

Through her efforts, Retention Concierge has partnered with 9 of the world's best Tech companies serving brands like Pepsi, Nike, Amazon, MTN, Dangote, Multichoice, Total, Sterling Bank, UBA, Wakanow, Mano, African Bank, JP Morgan and several others. Some of these technology companies include Moengage, Freshworks, Deel, Apollo.io, Paystack, Happay, and Xoxoday.

Jennifer is the founder of African Women in Technology Sales and the Convener of the TechSheroes conference through which women across the continent have the opportunity to acquire skills and practical experience in technology sales. Her goal is to build a formidable ecosystem of skilled Africans who can harness global opportunities in Techsales to improve not just their income but also drive national economies through foreign investment and the exchange of skill for value.

Scindia Balasingh

Head Of Global Marketing & Partnerships - Multiplier

Scindia Balasingh, an adept leader, demonstrates exceptional versatility, seamlessly navigating various domains. From spearheading the establishment of marketing functions to skilfully coordinating influential global partnerships, she excels in strategic marketing and showcases adaptability in maneuvering through intricate and evolving landscapes.

Embarking on a professional journey rooted in familial expectations for engineering, Scindia swiftly pivoted towards her true calling in technology communication and marketing during her MBA. From orchestrating pan-India marketing at Blue Star to spearheading the initiation of the marketing function at Redington, each role has been a stepping stone.

Upon joining Freshworks, Scindia immersed herself in the realm of global partnership marketing, collaborating with industry titans and leaving an indelible mark in the global partnership ecosystem. Currently serving as the Head Of Global Partner Marketing at Multiplier, She has emerged as a driving force within the realm of marketing. With a robust background and a wealth of experience in the dynamic landscape of technology marketing, she has carved a niche for herself as a forerunner in the field. A dynamic leader renowned for her strategic prowess in technology marketing, Scindia engages audiences as a speaker at esteemed forums like IIM Kashipur, IIT Kharagpur, SSN, World Women Economic Forum, G20 Summit, Umagine, CIO News, CXOTV and numerous other prestigious forms. She also judged the American speak-off idol.

Passionate about women's leadership and diversity, Scindia hosts the MOV LeadHERs podcast series, celebrating the achievements and contributions of women leaders.

Her awards & recognitions include being the winner of prestigious Asia's 100 Power Leaders in Marketing & Corporate Communications 2022, recognized by White Leadership Congress, and being honored as the Woman Icon of The Year (Marketing) at The 2022 Global Choice Award, also got recognized as an emerging CMO by the CMO Leadership Awards 2021, proud recipient of the Tech Marketer 2021 Award by CXOTV, celebrated as the Women Icon of The Year by the Women Economic Forum, and distinguished as Tamil Nadu's Top Woman Leader by the World Women Leadership Congress. She is acknowledged as a Top 20 Women Catalyst in the Indian IT Award by SME channels and received the Women Super Achiever Award from the World Women Leadership Congress. Scindia is also honored with the Women in Martech Award and is a recipient of the Global Women Icon Award bestowed by the World Women Economic Forum.
