About Us

African Women in Technology Sales ‘AWITS’ is an online community that connects African women from around the world who either are in tech sales or hope to pivot into tech sales.

This platform enables networking, learning, sharing, career advancement, and financial empowerment opportunities for African women.

Join the AWITS Mentorship Guild

The African Women in Technology Sales (AWITS) Guild is an exclusive, empowering community dedicated to training and mentoring African women to become top-tier professionals and entrepreneurs in tech sales, fostering leadership and economic influence across the continent

Criteria

  • Professional or Entrepreneurial Experience: Minimum of 5 years' experience in entrepreneurship or corporate roles within technology companies, ideally in sales, products, marketing, operations, or business development.
  • Commitment to Growth and Mentorship: Strong desire to learn, receive mentorship, and contribute to empowering others.
  • Availability for Engagement: Able to consistently commit time to mentorship, training, and community participation.
  • Vision for Impact: Clear goal to apply skills toward driving innovation, empowerment, and leadership in Africa’s tech sales space.
