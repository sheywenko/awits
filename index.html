About Us

African Women in Technology Sales ‘AWITS’ is an online community that connects African women from around the world who either are in tech sales or hope to pivot into tech sales.

This platform enables networking, learning, sharing, career advancement, and financial empowerment opportunities for African women.

AWITS leverages access to global technology partners through her affiliation with The Retention Concierge and these partnerships provide an excellent launching pad for women who either want to improve their income status through freelancing or to gain | or improve their experience in tech sales skills for career advancement purposes.
